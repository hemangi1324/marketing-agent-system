# Brand Guidelines — Acme SaaS

## Company
Acme SaaS helps small businesses automate their operations.
Founded 2022. HQ: San Francisco. 50 employees.

## Target Audience
- Small business owners (5–50 employees)
- Age: 30–50
- Pain points: Too much manual work, can't afford big enterprise tools
- Goals: Save time, grow without hiring, look professional

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Friendly, confident, no-nonsense
- **Personality**: Like a smart friend who happens to be a tech expert
- **Never**: Corporate jargon, overly technical, condescending
- **Always**: Clear, warm, action-oriented, outcomes-focused

## Key Messages
1. "Save 10 hours a week — guaranteed"
2. "Enterprise power, small business price"
3. "Your team of one just got a lot bigger"

## Products
- **Acme Automate**: Workflow automation ($29/mo)
- **Acme Connect**: CRM + email ($49/mo)
- **Acme Grow**: Full suite ($79/mo)

## Visual Identity
- Primary: #1a1a2e (deep navy)
- Accent: #e94560 (electric red)
- Light: #f5f5f5
- Font: Clean, modern sans-serif

## Content Pillars
1. 🎯 Productivity tips for small business
2. 🤖 AI & automation education
3. 💼 Customer success stories
4. 📊 Industry insights & trends
5. 🎉 Product updates & launches

## Hashtags
#SmallBusiness #Automation #Productivity #SaaS #WorkSmarter

## Competitors
- Zapier (more complex, expensive)
- Make.com (technical, steep learning curve)
- Our edge: Simplest setup, best support, SMB-focused
