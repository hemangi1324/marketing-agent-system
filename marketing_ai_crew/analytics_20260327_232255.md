**Recommendations for the Next 7 Days:**

The campaign is showing significant progress, particularly in driving clicks and reducing CPA, but the CTR is still shy of the 1% mark required for a "healed" campaign. Our focus must now be on pushing this last mile.

1.  **A/B Test High-Performing Ad Variations on Facebook & Instagram:** Identify the specific ad creatives (visuals, headlines, calls-to-action) that drove the most significant CTR improvements on Google Search and LinkedIn this past week. Replicate and test these winning elements, adapted for the visual nature of Facebook and Instagram, to improve relevance and click-through rates on these high-volume platforms. Specifically, iterate on visuals that clearly depict "saving 10 hours a week" or "enterprise power at SMB price" with Indian small business contexts.
2.  **Enhance Localised Testimonials and Social Proof on Landing Pages:** While the main landing page performs well, to convert more of the increased traffic, embed more prominent, culturally relevant customer success stories and testimonials directly on the `grow-smarter` page. Consider adding short video testimonials from Indian SMB owners demonstrating the "10 hours saved" claim in action to build trust and further reduce bounce rate on the Case Studies page.
3.  **Refine Google Search Ads Keyword Strategy to Capture Higher Intent:** Google Search Ads are outperforming. Conduct a deep dive into search query reports to identify exact match keywords that generated high CTRs and conversion rates. Increase bids on these specific, high-intent terms and consider expanding ad groups for very niche, long-tail keywords related to "affordable business automation India" or "CRM solutions for small businesses in [specific Indian city]" to capture even more qualified traffic.
